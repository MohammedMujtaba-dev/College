Courses We Offer

We offer the best fashion technology courses for students at Dhruva College of Fashion Technology.

BSc in Fashion Technology

This course provides students with a comprehensive understanding of fashion design, textile technology, and garment production.

Diploma in Fashion Design

A diploma program focusing on the fundamentals of fashion design, including sketching, pattern making, and garment construction.

MSc in Fashion Management

This course is designed for those looking to advance their careers in fashion management and marketing.

BBA in Fashion Design Management

The BBA in Fashion Design Management program integrates business administration with fashion design principles

Bachelor's in Fashion Technology

Our Bachelor's in Fashion Technology program stands out as the premier choice for budding fashion designers in Hyderabad.

BBA in Retail Fashion Management

Retail Fashion Management involves overseeing the operations and strategies of fashion retail businesses.

BBA in Luxury Brand Management

The BBA in Luxury Brand Management equips students to excel in the elite realm of luxury goods and services.

Bachelors in Interior Design

The Bachelor's program in Interior Design integrates business administration with interior design principles.
